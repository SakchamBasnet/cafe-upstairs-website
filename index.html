Our story

Above the street,
part of the city.

Cafe Upstairs sits one flight above the main road in Itahari, Sunsari — close enough to watch the traffic and the lights below, far enough to feel like you've stepped away from it.

Since 2015 we've poured everything from classic espresso to cold brews, blended seasonal fruit juices, and plated comfort food alongside menu staples — built around one idea: stay as long as you like.

Vinyl-warm lighting, framed prints, and a wall of stories make every table feel like the corner booth you come back to.
